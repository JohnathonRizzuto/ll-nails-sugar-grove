About L&L

A small studio doing big work.

Five chairs, dedicated stations, and a sanitization protocol we wrote out and posted on the wall so clients never have to ask. We take time on every set because shortcuts show up in two weeks.

Our regulars are Sugar Grove locals, Aurora commuters, and the occasional Yorkville drive-in. The booking calendar runs ahead by a few weeks for the senior techs - book early for special occasions.

Walk-ins welcome when chairs open up; Fresha is the fastest way to know who's free right now.
